Admission Information

We welcome applications for Nursery, Lower Primary, and Upper Primary classes. Our admissions process is simple and transparent, ensuring every child has the opportunity to thrive in our caring and dynamic environment.

  • Visit our school or contact us for a tour.
  • Fill out the admission form below or collect one from the school office.
  • Submit the completed form with a copy of the child's birth certificate and recent passport photo.
  • Attend an interview/assessment (for primary classes).
  • Receive admission decision and join our family!
